# Marketing pages (`/en/*` and `/ar/*`) are open to every crawler that honours
# robots.txt. Auth and authenticated surfaces are closed to everyone.
# AI-search crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, OAI-SearchBot,
# Google-Extended) are explicitly welcomed so Daris can be cited in AI
# answers to "best online Quran teacher" style queries.
